Achilles Last Stand was created in 1976, which was released in their opening track on their seventh studio album. The song itself was inspired by not only not being able to return to the UK, but also from local music heard in Morocco during the summer of 1975. One the sing was composed, it joined the rankes as one of their longest songs. The song itself was approximatly ten minutes and 22 seconds.

Top Rated Artist!

Eminem

Eminem, or Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, record executive and actor. Eminem started his career in 1988, with his group New Jacks. Since then he has made a big name for himself as Eminem, which has allotted him 15 Grammy Awards, 8 American Music Awards, 17 Billboard Music Awards, an Academy Award and a MTV Europe Music Global Icon Award.
